---
term: "Attention output (pre-projection)"
aliases: ["pre-W^O attention output", "head context", "concatenated head output"]
see: [multi-head-attention-output]
tags: [attention, shapes]
---

The context produced before the learned multi-head output projection $\mathbf W^O$. One head returns $\operatorname{head}_r\in\R^{n\times d_v}$; concatenating $h$ heads gives $\operatorname{Concat}(\operatorname{head}_1,\dots,\operatorname{head}_h)\in\R^{n\times hd_v}$. This object is not yet the multi-head sublayer's $d$-wide output.
