---
term: "Associative retrieval"
aliases: ["content-addressed lookup", "associative lookup"]
see: [in-context-learning, attention-head-output]
tags: [attention, memory, terminology]
---

Retrieval in which a query selects stored values by similarity to their keys rather than by an explicit numeric address. In softmax attention, a target key's score margin over distractors controls both its missing probability mass and the resulting value-retrieval error: the bounds decrease exponentially in the margin and grow with the number of competing memories.
