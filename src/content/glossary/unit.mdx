---
term: "Unit"
aliases: ["sequence unit", "raw unit"]
see: [token, discrete-code, image-patch]
tags: [inputs, terminology]
---

The modality-neutral raw element at one sequence position before the guide's embedding interface. A unit may be a text token, image patch, audio frame, or discrete code. The embedding map sends it to one row in $\R^d$; results stated over the resulting row sequence do not depend on which kind of unit produced it.
