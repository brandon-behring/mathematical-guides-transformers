---
term: "Compute-optimal training"
aliases: ["compute-optimal allocation", "Chinchilla-optimal training"]
see: [scaling-law, over-training]
tags: [training, scaling, optimization]
---

Allocation of a fixed training-compute proxy between model parameters and seen training tokens so as to minimize a specified fitted loss. The optimum depends on the fit, cost proxy, counting conventions, and constraints; changing the objective to include repeated serving cost can change the preferred allocation.
