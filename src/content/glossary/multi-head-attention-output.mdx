---
term: "Multi-head attention output (post-projection)"
aliases: ["post-W^O attention output", "MHA output"]
see: [attention-head-output]
tags: [attention, shapes]
---

The concatenated head contexts after multiplication by the learned output projection $\mathbf W^O\in\R^{hd_v\times d}$. It has shape $n\times d$ and is the object added to the residual stream. It should not be confused with a single $n\times d_v$ head context or the unprojected $n\times hd_v$ concatenation.
