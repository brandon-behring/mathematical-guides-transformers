---
term: "Resident parameters"
aliases: ["resident capacity", "parameter residency"]
see: [activated-arithmetic, active-expert-traffic]
tags: [systems, mixture-of-experts]
---

The full parameter capacity that must be addressable by the serving system, whether or not every parameter is used for the current input. For $E$ equal-size experts of $\mu_{\mathrm{FFN}}$ bytes each, expert residency is $E\mu_{\mathrm{FFN}}$. Residency does not by itself determine activated FLOPs or bytes fetched in one batch.
