---
term: "Subword"
aliases: ["subword token", "BPE token", "text piece"]
see: [token, unit]
tags: [inputs, tokenization, terminology]
---

A variable-length text piece used as one discrete token. A subword tokenizer keeps a base alphabet for unfamiliar strings expressible over that alphabet and adds larger pieces for recurring character sequences, trading vocabulary size against sequence length. A byte-pair encoding (BPE) piece need not coincide with a linguistic morpheme, and learned merges do not by themselves restore information discarded during normalization or pretokenization.
