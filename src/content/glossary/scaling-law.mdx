---
term: "Scaling law"
aliases: ["neural scaling law", "pretraining scaling law"]
see: [compute-optimal-training, over-training]
tags: [training, scaling, terminology]
---

An empirical fit describing how a measured quantity changes with resources such as parameter count, training tokens, or compute. In this guide, a pretraining scaling law models held-out next-token loss within a stated model family and experimental regime; it is not a universal architectural identity or a guarantee about downstream capabilities.
