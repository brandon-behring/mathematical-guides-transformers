---
term: "Image patch"
aliases: ["patch"]
see: [unit, token, discrete-code]
tags: [multimodal, inputs]
---

A spatial image region, usually a $p\times p\times c$ array, flattened and projected into one row of the model input. A raw patch is a continuous unit. Its projected row is often called a soft visual token, but it is not a discrete code unless a quantizer assigns it a finite-alphabet index.
