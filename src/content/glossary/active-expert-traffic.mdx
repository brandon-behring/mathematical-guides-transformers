---
term: "Active-expert traffic"
aliases: ["ideal weight traffic", "traffic"]
see: [resident-parameters, activated-arithmetic]
tags: [systems, roofline, mixture-of-experts]
---

The ideal lower-bound weight traffic for the distinct experts used by one served batch. If $\mathcal A_B$ is the union of routed experts, $A_B=|\mathcal A_B|$, and each expert occupies $\mu_{\mathrm{FFN}}$ bytes, the once-read idealization is $A_B\mu_{\mathrm{FFN}}$. Actual traffic may be higher because of cache capacity, tiling, repeated loads, or communication.
