---
term: "Exact computation"
aliases: ["exact", "function-preserving"]
see: [lossless-prefix-memory]
tags: [correctness, systems]
---

Equality to a stated reference computation under the stated arithmetic model. Prefix reuse and FlashAttention are exact in this sense: they reorganize work without changing the mathematical output. Exactness is a relation between computations; it does not imply that an intermediate representation preserves every detail of its input.
