---
term: "Activated arithmetic"
aliases: ["activated computation", "activated FLOPs"]
see: [resident-parameters, active-expert-traffic]
tags: [systems, mixture-of-experts]
---

The operations actually executed for a particular input or served batch. In a top-$k$ mixture-of-experts layer with $T$ routed rows, one expert call costing $\Phi_{\mathrm{FFN}}$ floating-point operations (FLOPs), and no dropped assignments, the idealized activated arithmetic is $Tk\Phi_{\mathrm{FFN}}$. If capacity drops $D$ assignments, it is instead $(Tk-D)\Phi_{\mathrm{FFN}}$. Activated arithmetic is neither the layer's total parameter capacity nor the number of weight bytes fetched.
