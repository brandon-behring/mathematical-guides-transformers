---
term: "Activated arithmetic"
aliases: ["activated computation", "activated FLOPs"]
see: [resident-parameters, active-expert-traffic]
tags: [systems, mixture-of-experts]
---

The operations actually executed for a particular input or served batch. In a top-$k$ mixture-of-experts layer with $T$ routed rows and one expert call costing $\Phi_{\mathrm{FFN}}$ FLOPs, the idealized activated arithmetic is $Tk\Phi_{\mathrm{FFN}}$. It is neither the layer's total parameter capacity nor the number of weight bytes fetched.
