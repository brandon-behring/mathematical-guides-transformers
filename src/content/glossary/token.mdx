---
term: "Token"
aliases: ["discrete token", "sequence token"]
see: [unit, discrete-code, image-patch]
tags: [inputs, terminology]
---

A discrete symbol or index from a finite vocabulary before embedding; by common shorthand, the word also names the corresponding embedded sequence row. Text subwords and serialized visual codes are tokens. A continuous image patch or connector output is a unit and may be called a **soft token**, but it is not a discrete vocabulary item.
