---
term: "Lossless prefix memory"
aliases: ["lossless", "lossless cache"]
see: [exact-computation, state]
tags: [correctness, inference]
---

A stored representation that retains all information required to reproduce the reference prefix computation. A full KV cache is lossless for ordinary causal-attention prefix reuse. Fixed-size recurrent states and selected or evicted KV subsets are generally lossy relative to dense attention, even when their own updates are evaluated exactly.
