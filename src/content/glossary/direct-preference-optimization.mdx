---
term: "Direct preference optimization"
aliases: ["DPO"]
see: [reinforcement-learning-from-human-feedback, reference-policy]
tags: [training, alignment, terminology]
---

A preference-fine-tuning objective that classifies an observed winner against a loser using the difference of their trainable-policy-to-reference log ratios. Under explicit Bradley–Terry, support, expressivity, comparison-connectivity, and population-optimization assumptions, its optimum matches the corresponding reward optimum with Kullback–Leibler (KL) regularization; finite-data training need not.
