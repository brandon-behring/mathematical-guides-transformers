---
term: "State"
aliases: ["model state", "recurrent state", "decode state"]
see: [lossless-prefix-memory]
tags: [recurrence, inference]
---

Information carried from one step to the next so future outputs can depend on the past. The concrete object is local: a recurrent neural network (RNN) hidden vector, a state-space model (SSM) state, a matrix-valued linear-attention state, or a growing key–value (KV) cache. “State” therefore names a role, not one universal tensor or one guarantee of losslessness.
