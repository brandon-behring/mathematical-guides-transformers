Reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF) is post-training that uses human feedback to update a policy. In the canonical language-model pipeline, preference comparisons train a scalar reward model, then a policy is updated to increase learned reward while controlling drift from a reference policy. Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) is common in that policy stage, but neither an explicit reward model nor PPO defines every RLHF method.
