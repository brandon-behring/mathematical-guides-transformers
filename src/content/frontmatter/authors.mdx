# Authors

A formal, Definition–Theorem–Proof treatment of sequence models — from recurrent networks and
state-space models through attention and the transformer to hybrid architectures and vision–language —
part of the [mathematical-guides](https://github.com/brandon-behring/mathematical-guides) family,
authored under the family's
[formal style guide](https://github.com/brandon-behring/mathematical-guides/blob/main/docs/style-guide-formal-v0.3.md).

**Brandon Behring** — applied math PhD; author. The transformer core is ported and re-expressed from
the LaTeX `transformer_mathematics` guide; the recurrent, state-space, and hybrid-architecture arc is
new to this edition, in the family's motivated formal style.
